About Me
My passions and personality

One of the guiding tenents of my life has been Aristotle's idea of eudaimonia, or the idea that the path to happiness is best traveled by identifying the things you are talented at and concentrating on achieving the highest excellence in their practice. So, I choose to concentrate on a handful of activities and people in my life that I am truly passionate about.

One of those things is creating high quality software. As a lifelong perfectionist and technophile, I feel truly lucky to have found my calling in life and take genuine joy in the work. I believe that joy and pride is reflected in the things I make. As any perfectionist knows, being a perfectionist is both a blessing and a curse - it provides you with great attention to detail, which is a crucial trait in software engineering, but can sometimes cause you to get hung up on the beauty of the solution at the expense of the pragmatic implementation. Also, being a technophile, I love learning new tools and more efficient ways to build things, but the best tool for the job is the best tool for the job - whether it's new and exciting or tried and true. I do my best to be conscientious of the pitfalls of these traits and try to incorporate only the best parts into my methodologies.

Another thing I'm passionate about is seeing the world. I've always had a deep-seated curiousity about how other people do things - observing and taking what I judge to be the best differences and incorporating them into my own life - and what better way to do that than to travel the world? Also, let's be honest, our profession is planted in front of computer screens for the better part of a day: seeking out adventure is a great way to rejuvenate the soul. I've been to 23 countries on 4 continents and I'm on a quest to visit 50+ countries and to set foot on every continent on Earth.

I'm also passionate about fitness of body and mind. Our bodies and our minds are inextricably tied together - it is my firm belief that if one is unhealthy, then the other cannot possibly achieve its fullest potential. A healthy body is an active body, and a healthy mind is an active mind that is always absorbing and learning a variety of new things.

Finally, for me, photography goes hand in hand with travelling. I love going to interesting places and attempting to capture the essence of those places in still photographs. Over the years, it has become my primary artistic outlet. I've included a few of my best photographs below.
