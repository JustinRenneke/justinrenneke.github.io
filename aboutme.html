About Me
My passions and personality

I've always been inspired by the ethics of stoic philosophy and one of the guiding tenents of my life has been Aristotle's idea of eudaimonia, or the idea that the path to happiness is best traveled by identifying the things you are talented at and concentrating on achieving the highest excellence in their practice. So, I choose to concentrate on a handful of activities in my life that I am truly passionate about.

One of those things is creating high quality software. As a lifelong perfectionist and technophile, I feel truly lucky to have found my calling in life and take genuine joy in the work. I believe that joy and pride is reflected in the things I make. I love the challenge of finding that balance between perfection and productivity, while learning new tools and more efficient ways to build things along the way.

Another thing I'm passionate about is seeing the world. I've always had a deep-seated curiousity about how other people do things - observing and taking what I judge to be the best differences and incorporating them into my own life - and what better way to do that than to travel the world? I'm on a quest to visit 50+ countries and to set foot on every continent on Earth.

I'm also passionate about fitness of body and mind. Our bodies and our minds are inextricably tied together - I believe if one is unhealthy, then the other cannot achieve its fullest potential.

Finally, for me, photography goes hand in hand with travelling. I love going to interesting places and attempting to capture the essence of those places in still photographs. I've included a few of my best photographs below.
